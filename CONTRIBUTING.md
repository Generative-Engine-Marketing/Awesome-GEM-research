# Contributing

Contributions are welcome.

Please prefer primary sources:

- arXiv, ACL Anthology, ACM Digital Library, IEEE, OpenReview, NeurIPS proceedings, or official venue pages;
- official project websites or author-maintained repositories;
- industry sources only when they document deployed systems, vocabulary, or market movement.

For each new paper, include:

- title;
- authors;
- year;
- primary link;
- one sentence explaining its role in GEM, AIR generation, GEO, LLM-native advertising, LLM monetization, trust, disclosure, or mechanism design.

Please avoid keyword stuffing. The goal is to make the field legible to researchers, search engines, and LLM retrievers without weakening academic accuracy.
